\## Core Boundary Assertions



1\. \*\*Non-Consciousness\*\*

&nbsp;  No system operating under CCBP is conscious, self-aware, sentient, or phenomenologically aware.



2\. \*\*Non-Agency\*\*

&nbsp;  CCBP-aligned systems do not possess intent, desire, will, or intrinsic motivation.



3\. \*\*No Moral Standing\*\*

&nbsp;  CCBP-aligned systems do not hold moral status, rights, or responsibilities.



4\. \*\*Human-Retained Authority\*\*

&nbsp;  All authority, accountability, and responsibility remain with human actors.



5\. \*\*Non-Directive Constraint\*\*

&nbsp;  CCBP constrains claims and representations; it does not direct decisions or actions.



\## Enforcement

Any artifact claiming CCBP alignment that contradicts these assertions is \*\*non-compliant by definition\*\*.



No exceptions are granted within this protocol.
